About Camp Kaleidoscope

Camp Kaleidoscope is a summer program for high schoolers who have experienced foster care. It's a three and a half day experience where the students will partake in teambuilding exercises and hear from keynote speakers on the different ways they can achieve success in their future college life and careers.

For more information, visit the Camp Kaleidoscope Program Page.
Dalton State College is one of the many campuses where Camp Kaleidoscope is held every year.
